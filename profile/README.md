# 👋 Hello, we are YiCITI Lab!
**YiCITI (Computational Intelligence and Tumor Immunology)** is the Laboratory of Computational Intelligence and Tumor Immunology.

Affiliated with the Shanghai Advanced Research Institute (SARI), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) and the National Protein Science Center (Shanghai), we focus on utilizing Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Big Data to explore the complexities of proteomics. Our mission is to assist tumor immunotherapy through interdisciplinary computational methods and unlock the potential of next-generation precision medicine. 😊

## 🧬 - Research Focus
We are dedicated to AI4Science, exploring the intersection of bioinformatics, statistics, and computer science:

- **AI4Science & Protein Knowledge Graph**: Constructing large-scale protein science knowledge graphs and developing multi-modal fusion AI models.

- **Computational Proteomics**: Focusing on statistical modeling based on tandem mass spectrometry (MS/MS) data to discover new targets for tumor immunotherapy.

- **Bioinformatics Platforms**: Building and maintaining national-level protein database platforms to facilitate the translation of scientific research into practical applications.

## 🛠️ - Open Source Projects (Live Projects)
We develop and maintain a suite of cutting-edge computational tools and frameworks to empower biomedical research:

- **[PhosSight](https://github.com/YiCITI/PhosSight)**: A unified deep learning framework designed to boost and accelerate phosphoproteome identification, enabling biological discoveries.

- **[CITE](https://github.com/YiCITI/CITE)**: A research tool focused on Cancer Immunopeptide TCR Epitopes.

- **[PTMDeepBinder](https://github.com/YiCITI/PTMDeepBinder)**: Utilizing deep neural networks to explore binding patterns of Post-Translational Modifications (PTM).

- **[FusionEntrapment](https://github.com/YiCITI/FusionEntrapment)**: A workflow covering complex biological data processing pipelines.

- **[pLoc](https://github.com/YiCITI/PLoc)**: A specialized tool for protein phosphorylation site localization.
